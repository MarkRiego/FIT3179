An Electric Vehicle Engineering Masterclass

Named after the late inventor Nikola Tesla, Tesla Inc. exists to fulfill the mission of accelerating the world's transition to sustainable energy usage. Lead by Chief Executive Officer and Technoking Elon Mu​​sk, Tesla sold more electric vehicles than Ford, General Motors and Volkswagen combined in the year 2022. Their vehicles are known for their speed, unique driver technology, energy efficiency and relative affordability.

A Best-Selling Vehicle Lineup

Through hard work, the Tesla team has been able to manufacture and deliver several millions of vehicles worldwide. Tesla has stayed consistent with its targeted vehicle production growth rate of 50%, driven by its mission to provide increasingly affordable electric mobility to the masses.

Industry-Leading Technology

Tesla Inc. has attracted the brightest engineers from around the world to engineer its vehicles and the machines that manufacture them. These engineers have several patents under their names as Tesla employees in a variety of automotive engineering disciplines including battery technology, autonomous driving and vehicle manufacturing.

Long Vehicle Range

Tesla consistently stands out in EPA range ratings. With cutting-edge battery technology and unwavering dedication to efficiency, all Tesla models consistently achieve some of the highest range ratings in the industry. From the affordable Model 3 to the luxurious Model S and the versatile Model Y, Tesla's commitment to pushing the boundaries of electric mobility is evident. As we move towards a more sustainable future, Tesla's exceptional range performance underscores its dedication to providing practical and convenient electric vehicles for everyday drivers, making them a leader in the field.

A Global Charging Network

Aside from achieving large scale electric vehicle production, Tesla's most significant achievement thus far is the establishment of its own vehicle charging network worldwide. Several legacy automakers, including Ford, Mercedes and Nissan, have signed commercial contracts with Tesla to allow owners of their vehicles to use the Tesla Supercharger Network.
